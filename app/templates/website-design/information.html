Many Hats Website Design:

Modern, Efficient, Mobile-Friendly and Effective Website Designs.

Hi, my name is Sam Ritchie.

I'm a semi-professional contract web designer and developer, with a simple set of design goals that I apply to all of my projects.

I aim to:

  • Make fast, responsive websites. Nearly half of your users will expect your website to load in two seconds[1][2]. By following best practises, I will ensure that even the most impatient of users will find browsing your site a breeze.
  • Make stylish, modern websites. I take pride in designing websites that appeal to their demographic. I believe that the simplistic, clean and intuitive design of modern websites is the best place to start concerning all users of the internet. As such, I use this as a baseline when working with clients to develop a website that your customers will find attractive.
  • Make accessible websites. I feel that all websites should cater to all users. As I can (unfortunately) only influence the websites that I personally work on, I ensure that my websites are not only accessible but ideal for PCs, laptops, mobile devices, outdated browsers, and screen readers. As such, I will try my best to apply this same rule to your website.
  • Make unchallenging websites. I believe there is a limit to how stylish you should make a website. While a beautiful website is very important for gaining the attention of your user, it is overall pointless if the site is difficult to read! By using humanist fonts, vertical rhythms[3], correct grammar and correct spelling, I aim to make your user's experience as positive as possible.

...but that's just regarding the project itself.

I enjoy working with real people as well as code, and so when working with clients I aim to be friendly, diplomatic, truthful, and easy to contact (all without sacrificing professionalism of course).

If you'd like to start the conversation, feel free to visit the contact me page to get a quote!
