Modern, Efficient, Mobile-Friendly and Effective Website Designs.

Hi, my name is Sam Ritchie.

I'm a semi-professional contract web designer and developer, with a simple set of design goals that I apply to all of my projects.

I aim to make:

  • Fast, responsive websites. Nearly half of your users will expect your website to load in two seconds[1][2]. By following best practises, I will ensure that even the most impatient of users will find browsing your site a breeze.
  • Accessible websites. I feel that all websites should cater to all users. As I can (unfortunately) only influence the websites that I personally work on, I ensure that my websites are not only accessible but ideal for PCs, laptops, mobile devices, outdated browsers, and screen readers.
  • Stylish, modern websites. I take pride in designing websites that appeal to their demographic. I believe that the simplistic, clean and intuitive nature of modern websites is the best place to start concerning all users of the internet.
  • Unchallenging websites. I believe there is a limit to how stylish you should make a website. By using humanist fonts, vertical rhythms[3] and excellent proof-reading, I aim to make your user's experience as positive as possible.

but that's just regarding the project itself.

I enjoy working with real people as well as code, and so when working with clients I aim to be friendly, diplomatic, genuine, truthful, and easy to contact (all without sacrificing professionalism of course).
